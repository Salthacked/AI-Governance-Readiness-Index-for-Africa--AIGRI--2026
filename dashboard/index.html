Index Weighting & PCA Methodology

Adhering to OECD Guidelines for composite index construction, avoiding arbitrary weights.

Why Principal Component Analysis (PCA)?

Standard readiness indices (e.g. Oxford Insights, Tortoise) often use subjective equal weighting. We apply PCA to the four standardized pillars to mathematically define weights based on their contribution to the total variance of the readiness dimensions.

Our empirical model shows that the First Principal Component (PC1) explains 84.5% of the total variance across the 30 countries, proving that the index represents a highly cohesive socio-technical construct.
